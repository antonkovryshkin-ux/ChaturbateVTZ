Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new timezone?

Navigate to the "Time Map" tab and tap the green "+" button (or search bar). Type the name of the city you want to add and select it from the list.

Is my data private?

Yes. We do not require you to create an account. Your preferences are linked to your device using a secure, anonymous identifier (IDFV). Check our Privacy Policy for more details.

How does the "Overlap Finder" work?

The app analyzes the timezones you have selected and calculates the best time window where the overlap is "Daytime" for the majority of the selected regions. This is perfect for scheduling global video calls.

The app is crashing, what should I do?

Please ensure you are using the latest version of the app. If the problem persists, delete and reinstall the app (your settings should be safe). If that doesn't work, contact us via email.
