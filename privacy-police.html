Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

Welcome to Chaturbate VTZ. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to improve your experience and ensure the app functions correctly:

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and language/region settings.
  • Identifier for Vendor (IDFV): A unique identifier assigned to your device to store your preferences and settings securely without requiring a personal account.
  • Timezone Data: Your current timezone and the timezones you choose to track within the app.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data regarding app performance and crash reports (via Firebase).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To synchronization your saved timezones across potential future updates.
  • Personalization: To verify your region for "Smart Overlap" suggestions (Prime Time calculations).
  • Analytics: To understand user demographics (country, device type) and improve app stability.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to securely handle data:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore): Used for secure cloud storage of your preferences and IDFV.
  • Google Firebase (Remote Config): Used to keep app policies and features up to date without app store updates.

These services adhere to strict security standards (GDPR & CCPA compliant).

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. We do not sell or trade your personal data to third parties.

5. Contact Us

Email: support@wbibi.com
